FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
How does stress monitoring work? ▼
TRANQUIL simulates stress monitoring based on your interactions. In a real implementation, it would connect to wearable devices that track heart rate, HRV, and skin temperature. The demo generates realistic stress patterns to demonstrate the app's functionality.
Is my data private and secure? ▼
Yes! All your data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. No data is sent to any server. You can export your data anytime from Settings, and you have full control to delete it whenever you want.
What are the breathing techniques? ▼
4-7-8 Breathing: Inhale 4 seconds, hold 7, exhale 8 - great for anxiety.
Box Breathing: Equal 4-second phases - used by Navy SEALs for focus.
Deep Calm: 5-second inhale and exhale - perfect for relaxation.
Can I use this without an internet connection? ▼
Absolutely! TRANQUIL works completely offline once loaded. All features including stress tracking, journaling, and breathing exercises work without internet.
How do I export my data? ▼
Go to Settings → Data & Privacy → Export My Data. You'll get a JSON file with all your wellness data that you can backup or analyze externally.
What do the stress scores mean? ▼
0-30: Low Stress - You're doing great!
31-60: Medium Stress - Consider relaxation techniques
61-100: High Stress - Take a break, try breathing exercises
💡 Pro Tip

Combine breathing exercises with nature sounds for maximum relaxation. Try the 4-7-8 technique while listening to rain sounds!
